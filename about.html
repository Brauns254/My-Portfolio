About Me

Hi, I’m Brauns Brian, a passionate educator and aspiring software engineer based in the vibrant heart of Nairobi, Kenya. By day, I inspire students, sparking curiosity and guiding them with knowledge and patience. By night, I’m a dreamer crafting code, blending logic and creativity to build innovative digital solutions.

With a knack for creative design and skills in HTML, CSS, and problem-solving, I’m on a mission to bridge education and technology, creating impactful solutions that resonate. Whether it’s writing insightful blogs, designing sleek websites, or exploring new tech, I thrive on turning ideas into reality.

When I’m not teaching or coding, you’ll find me diving into Nairobi’s dynamic energy or brainstorming content for my next project. Let’s connect to create something extraordinary!
